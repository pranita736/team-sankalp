Aptitude Solving

Aptitude solving is a basic skill required for the career opportunities. We conduct aptitude tests and answering sessions to work on the aptitude solving skills. Here we discuss different approaches towards the answer.

Daily Hustle

It is a session started to motivate students work daily inorder to achieve their short-term and the long-term goals. We've a daily standup culture, which will be followed in the corporate world.

Speak To Lead

Public speaking is a very essential skill nowadays. In order to improve our public speaking skills we conduct Speak To Lead sessions. This provides a platform for the members of the team to bring out their ideas and improve their skills.

Technophilia

We're in the era of technology, being an engineering student we should have some knowledge about coding, and problem solving. We share the resources for learning coding, internships available, and motivate them to start working in groups.
